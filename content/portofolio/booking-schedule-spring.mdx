## Business Logic

1. Sign Up & Login
    Users can create an account and log in to access the app.

2. Email Verification
    After signing up, users must verify their email address to activate their account.

3. Room Booking
    Users can browse and book available hotel rooms.

4. Check-in & Check-out
    When arriving at the hotel, users can check in digitally.
    Upon departure, they can check out through the app.

5. Forgot Password
    If users forget their password, they can reset it via a link sent to their email.

6. Room Management (Admin Only)
    Admins can add, update, or remove rooms from the system.
